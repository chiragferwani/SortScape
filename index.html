Bubble Sort

Bubble Sort is an iterative sorting algorithm that imitates the movement of bubbles in sparkling water. The bubbles represent the elements of the data structure. The bigger bubbles reach the top faster than smaller bubbles, and this algorithm works in the same way.

  • Average Complexity: O(n2)
  • Best Case: O(n)
  • Worst Case: O(n2)
  • Space Complexity: O(1)
